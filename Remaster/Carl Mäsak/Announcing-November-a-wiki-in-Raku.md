# Announcing November, a wiki in Raku
    
*Originally published on [17 August 2008](http://strangelyconsistent.org/blog/announcing-november-a-wiki-in-perl-6) by Carl Mäsak.*

We're pleased to annouce the release of November, a wiki engine written in Raku.

November is:

- ...a proof-of-concept of what Rakudo Raku can do today.
- ...released early rather than "when it's done".
- ...meant to promote interest and involvement in Raku/Rakudo/Parrot development.
