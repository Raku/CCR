My trip to Moscow was a pleasant one,

- The city has a certain feel I like.
- The metro is so big and full of win!
- And so too was [the room](https://irclogs.raku.org/perl6/2010-05-16.html#21:48-0001) at my hotel.
- [My talk](https://masak.org/carl/devconf-2010/talk.pdf) was well-received and fun to give, on Yapsi, Tardis, Sigmund and Raku.
- [sharifulin](https://x.com/sharifulin)++ for speedy help with [setting up his laptop](https://twitpic.com/1ol9ms) for my talk.
- The guys at [Moscow.pm](http://moscow.pm.org/) made me feel at home in Moscow, and enjoy my stay.
- I only wish [my Russian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_language) wasn't crap.  Incentive to learn more of it, I guess.
- At [Airport Sheremetyevo](https://www.svo.aero/en/main) they said:
 "departure is tomorrow, not today".  'Pro tip': when booking, check an extra time that chosen dates are actually correct.
