# "Final report" for Mozilla Foundation and TPF grant
    
*Originally published on [11 September 2008](https://use-perl.github.io/user/pmichaud/journal/37403/) by Patrick Michaud.*

Last year I received a [Raku development grant](https://news.perlfoundation.org/post/patrick_michaud_awarded_perl_6) from the [Mozilla Foundation](https://www.mozillafoundation.org/en/) and [The Perl Foundation](https://www.perlfoundation.org).  Below is a copy of the final report I've submitted to close out the grant.  It's also available in [PDF](https://www.pmichaud.com/perl6/mfp6grant.pdf) if anyone wants a more printable version.

My thanks to everyone who helped me with this grant, and I'm looking forward to the next one.
